Water Bills

Pre-Handoff Checklist Water Bills
Step 1 Process the Bill
  1. Open the Main Water Bill Tracking Sheet & pick a bill date today.
  2. DPW Portal search property download latest bill.
  3. Name Owner Copy: YYYYMMDD_Property_$Amount_dueMMDD.
  4. Duplicate Tenant Copy (remove owner; highlight balances).
  5. If previous cycle unpaid add 5% late fee in Buildium (WB Late Fee Income).
  6. Post current bill to tenant ledger (Account: Utility Income) and attach Tenant Copy.
Step 2 Prepare the Bill
  1. Upload Owner Copy (Owner only) & Tenant Copy (Tenant only) to Buildium.
  2. Send notices (Email + 1st Notice). Save as YYYYMMDD_Property_WaterBillNotice.
  3. Upload PDFs to the designated Drive folder.
  4. Email the Water Bill Process Report to: rachel@, michelle@, neha@
Step 3 Pay the Bill

Rule Summary: Pay $200 directly. Over $200: confirm no unpaid balances & compare to 6-mo avg. Escalate if >$300 (SFD) or >$350 (MFD).
